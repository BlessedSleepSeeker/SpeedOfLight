# Formal Future
Formal Future is a 15pt pixel art font designed to have a sharp, boxy and futuristic feel.

## Author
[VEXED](https://v3x3d.itch.io/)

## Basic Info
- Base Glyph Height: 15px
- Scales: 15, 30, 45, 60, etc
- Ascender Max: 7px
- Descender Max: 4px

## Kerning
None
